# Branding & UI Design Specification

## Product Identity
- **Name:** Annonai Flow
- **Tagline:** "Conversations that convert." / "Fluidez para tu negocio."
- **Concept:** Seamless movement from chat -> sale -> payment.

---

## Visual Design System

### Color Palette
- **Annonai Midnight:** `#0F172A` (Rich blue-grey for backgrounds/headers)
- **Flow Teal:** `#0D9488` (Primary Brand Color)
- **WhatsApp Action:** `#22C55E` (Exclusive for WhatsApp integration)
- **Success/Money:** `#10B981` (Emerald)
- **Pending/Action:** `#F59E0B` (Amber)
- **Error/Danger:** `#EF4444` (Red)

### Typography
- **Headings:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` or `Outfit`
- **Body/UI:** `Inter` or `Geist Sans`
- **Monospaced:** `JetBrains Mono` or `Fira Code` (For IDs and invoice numbers)

---

## Design Language: "Fluid Glass"
- **Glassmorphism:** Use `backdrop-blur-md` on cards and modals.
- **Shadows:** Deep, diffuse shadows for depth.
- **Corners:** Rounded-2xl (1rem+) for cards and main containers.
- **Interactions:** Subtle scale animations on hover (`scale-102`).

---

## Iconography
- **Library:** `Lucide React`
- **Stroke Width:** 1.5px - 2px
- **Style:** Minimalist, rounded.

---

## Tone of Voice
- **Approachable:** Use natural language (e.g., "Hola, aquí está tu factura").
- **Encouraging:** Celebrate successes (e.g., "¡Gran venta!").
- **Clear:** Zero technical jargon for the end-user.
